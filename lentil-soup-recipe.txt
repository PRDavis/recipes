 2 tbs evo
 1 lb beef roast, 1-in cubes
 salt and pepper to taste
 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
 1 lg onion, chopped
 4 cloves garlic, chopped
 2 teaspoons dry basil
 2 teaspoons dried cilantro
 2 teaspoon dried oregano
 2 (32 ounce) cartons chicken broth
 2 cups dry lentils
 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
 3 stalks celery, chopped
 2 carrots, chopped

 brown beef in olive oil
 remove beef and add vegetables to oil - cook for 7 min or until translucent

add beef, tomatoes and chicken broth, cover and simmer for 1 hour
add lentils, cover and simmer for 40 min or until lentils are tender
